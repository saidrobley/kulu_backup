Kulu development Organization is a nonprofit agency that is dedicated to the welfare of the people living in Nuba Mountains, Sudan. It is registered in the state of Colorado and federally under section 501(C)(3) of the U.S. department of Internal Revenue Service (IRS). Kulu was founded in 2017 by a group of individuals from Nuba Mountains who live abroad and are timely and deeply heartfelt for the well being of their families, relatives and friends in the war zones.

Nourishment - Empowerment - Robustness

OUR PRODUCTIVE FARM

Despite the constant horrifying sounds of government fighter jets and Antonov aircrafts overhead, the local populations are determined to cultivate crops in an attempt to feed their families. However, farmers lack the equipment needed to sustain their farming practices. We certainly plan to provide this agrarian society with the tools to help them grow crops and breed livestock. Food security in the Nuba Mountains is our main project. The government uses food as a weapon to deteriorate the situation in the region.

GRASS-ROOFED CLASSROOM

Children in the war-torn areas of Sudan have become accustomed to attending schools in grass-roofed shelters, caves and under the trees. These facilities tend to be cramped, holding hundreds of students and offering little protection. One of our main goals is to rebuild this classroom and many more to increase access to fundamental education for unfortunate kids who are affected by the conflict. Even though the environment and atmosphere are not conducive, children in Nuba Mountains have never given up education.
